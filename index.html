Pensieri Verticali

Morton Feldman, Adelphi editions
Ba thesis project, mentor Mario Piazza

Visual re-edition of the book ‘Pensieri Verticali’, by the composer Morton Feldman. This book presents a vast collection of the composer’s essays and three notes by other authors, included in the form of attachments on the third cover. The project aims to reveal the intellectual and contemplative nature of Feldman’s music through a carefully composed typography.

Impulsi

Logo design for a political party

The party logo project began as a means to produce a robust and recognizable brand, that filled the lack of symbols in the politics of Italy today.

A representation in design of the epicentre of an earthquake and the symbol for WiFi were my iconographic influences, they translate meaning into an elementary language that is easy for all to understand.

On Typography

and the spread of modern thought (very briefly)

Language has its origins in oral communication and it only finds written form later in history. Occidental thinking is based on the practice of writing, as Walter J. Ong states in his essay Orality and Literacy, and typography has always been the discipline through which writing finds its optimal form and clarity. Therefore the noble technology of typography has had a fundamental importance in giving birth to Modernity, intended as the product of occidental thinking.

Indil jewelry

Handmade with the technique of soutache

Brand identity project for a jewelry artisan who specialises in the technique of soutache. The request of the client was to communicate the sinuosity that distinguish her jewelry through a new logotype, which has been later declined on various communicative products such as business cards or packagings.

Feiern

Visual trailer of the documentary by Maja Classen

Feiern is a documentary about Berlin’s trasgressive club culture, released in 2005. The printed content of the pages develops as a tale about the nights spent here: from the dark entrance of the club, to the sunrise the next morning that breaks in the dancefloor. The small size of the 16 pages pamphlet reflects the size of the flyers that were disseminated throught the city to advertise such parties.

WOW

Logo design for a new Milanese communication agency

Its debatable name was concieved by the founders to be visually translated into the acronym: WOW. This was the main point of the client’s brief, and the whole work aims to highlight such sense of surprise. The client asked for a discreet design, therefore the project tends to enlight the classical idea of logo, using only typographic elements that immediately recall the agency’s full name.
